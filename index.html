Our Story

Founded in 1998 by the Johnson family, GreenHarvest began as a small organic farm with a simple mission: to provide communities with the freshest, healthiest vegetables while preserving the environment for future generations.

25+ Years of Experience

What started as a 5-acre plot has grown into a 200-acre sustainable farm operation, We've maintained commitment to organic practices never using synthetic pesticides herbicides. or GMOs. Our soil is enriched naturally through crop rotation, composting, and beneficial insect programs.
